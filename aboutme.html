About me

I am a London-based UX Specialist with over five years of professional experience delivering user-centered solutions in fast-paced, international environments. With a background in Business Administration, I bring a strong understanding of how to align user needs with business objectives to drive measurable outcomes.

My expertise spans the full UX lifecycle, including user research, information architecture, usability testing, and data-driven design. I have led and supported end-to-end UX initiatives, applying qualitative and quantitative methodologies to identify pain points and deliver effective, scalable solutions.

I am experienced in collaborating with cross-functional teams and stakeholders, facilitating workshops, and translating complex requirements into clear, actionable insights. My technical knowledge in HTML, CSS, and SQL enables effective communication with development teams and supports the delivery of design solutions.
